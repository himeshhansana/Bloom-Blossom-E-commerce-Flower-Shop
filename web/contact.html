FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our flowers, delivery, and services

Do you offer same-day delivery?
Yes! We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM. We cover all major areas in Mumbai and surrounding regions.
How fresh are your flowers?
Our flowers are hand-picked daily from our partner farms and stored in climate-controlled environments. We guarantee 7-day freshness.
Can you create custom arrangements?
Absolutely! Our expert florists love creating custom arrangements. Contact us with your vision and we'll bring it to life.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and cash on delivery for your convenience.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your flowers, contact us within 24 hours for a replacement or refund.
How far in advance can I place an order?
You can place orders up to 30 days in advance. For special occasions like weddings, we recommend booking 2-4 weeks ahead.
